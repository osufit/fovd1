This website is about CodeChum, an effective platform for learning programming in the ICT strand at Tapinac Senior High School. It provides interactive lessons, hands-on coding exercises, and real-time feedback, making it easier for students to understand complex programming concepts. With its user-friendly interface and structured learning path, CodeChum helps students develop problem-solving skills and coding proficiency. Teachers can also track student progress and provide guidance, ensuring an engaging and efficient learning experience. By using CodeChum, ICT students at Tapinac Senior High School can build a strong foundation in programming, preparing them for future careers in technology.
